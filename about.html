Interdisciplinary Art Exhibition

The Interdisciplinary Exhibition is an innovative and transformative event that brings together professionals, experts, and enthusiasts from various fields to explore the intersection of art and technology in a different cities for every 2 years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and individuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge. So if you're active and engaged in the worlds of open content and knowledge - free software advocates, wikipedians, Open knowledge, galleries, libraries, museums, archives, governments and foundations, lawyers, and activists - we hope you'll consider joining us this year to build a stronger, more vibrant commons together.
