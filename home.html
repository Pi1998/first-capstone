Interdisciplinary Art Exhibition
Myanmar
2023

We are delighted to extend our heartfelt invitation to you for an extraordinary event that celebrates the convergence of diverse fields and the evolution of art.
Join us at our Interdisciplinary Exhibition, where the boundaries of creativity are transcended, and new horizons are explored.

2023.11.1(FRI) ~ 3(SUN)

@Peoples' Park, Yangon

Main Program

    • Lecture

    • This exceptional exhibition brings together esteemed speakers from various fields, each with their unique expertise and perspective.

    • Art Exhibition

    • Immerse yourself in a world of artistic wonder as you witness a remarkable showcase of artworks that embody the fusion of creative disciplines.

    • Forum

    • Participants will have the opportunity to actively contribute to the discussions, share their perspectives, ask questions, and gain insights from the diverse range of voices in attendance.

    • Workshop

    • The workshop sessions provide a unique opportunity about practical aspects of interdisciplinary art and gain valuable insights from experts in their respective fields.

    • Network

    • This is the perfect setting to expand your horizons and establish connections that can enrich your creative endeavors.
